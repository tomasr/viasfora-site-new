About

Viasfora is a free extension for Visual Studio 2010 - 2015 that improves your text editing experience through the use of color and other features. Viasfora is mostly known for implementing Rainbow Braces for Visual Studio languages!

Supported languages include: C#, C/C++, VB and VBScript, F#, JavaScript, TypeScript, CSS/LESS/SASS, SQL, PowerShell, Python, and R.
